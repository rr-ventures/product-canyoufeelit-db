## Stage 1: YouTube (Awareness)

**Objective**: Get someone to click play

**Key elements**:
- **Thumbnail**: Emotion + visual intrigue (not generic)
- **Title**: Specific feeling + genre (optimized for search)
- **First 15 seconds**: Best moment of the mix (hook them immediately)

**Conversion goal**: 10% CTR (click-through rate from impressions)

---

## Stage 2: Video Experience (Engagement)

**Objective**: Keep them listening long enough to care

**Key elements**:
- **Track quality**: Every song passes the "2am test"
- **DJ craft**: Smooth transitions, no dead air
- **Tracklist**: Timestamped so they can find gems
- **Description**: Emotional hook in first 2 sentences

**Conversion goal**: 50%+ average watch time

## Stage 6: Ongoing Engagement (Retention)

**Objective**: Keep them engaged so they stay subscribed and become superfans

### Email (weekly or fortnightly):
- 3 track finds with emotional context
- Personal reflection on the theme
- Soft mention of new mix (if uploaded recently)
- Question at the end to prompt replies

**Churn goal**: <5% unsubscribe rate

### Discord (ongoing):
- Share mix previews before YouTube upload
- Ask for feedback on track selection
- Host "track of the week" discussions
- Celebrate milestones (100 subs, 1K views, etc.)

**Engagement goal**: 20-30% of members active weekly

## Stage 7: Advocacy (Growth Loop)

**Objective**: Turn superfans into advocates who share your work

**Tactics**:
- End each email with: "Know someone who'd vibe with this? Forward it."
- Pin Discord message: "Invite a friend who gets it"
- Feature listener track recommendations in mixes (with credit)
- Create "listener-requested mix" (crowd-sourced theme)

**Conversion goal**: 10-20% of engaged audience shares your content

## Optimization Playbook (After 3+ Uploads)

### If YouTube CTR is low (<5%):
- Test new thumbnail style
- Test emotion-first vs vibe-first titles
- Improve first 15 seconds of mix (stronger hook)

### If watch time is low (<40%):
- Review track selection (are they actually gems?)
- Check transitions (are they smooth or jarring?)
- Shorten mixes (20min may perform better than 30min)

### If landing page CTR is low (<3%):
- Make pinned comment more compelling
- Add end screen CTA
- Test different CTA copy in description

### If email signup rate is low (<20%):
- Simplify landing page (fewer words, clearer value prop)
- Add social proof
- Test "Join Discord" as primary CTA instead

### If email open rate drops (<30%):
- Test different subject lines (more personal, less formal)
- Check send time (Fri/Sat evenings may work better)
- Audit content quality (are you delivering on the promise?)

**The funnel is not about manipulation. It's about deepening the relationship with people who already vibe with your work.**

If someone doesn't sign up, that's fine. You gave them a great mix. That's enough.

The ones who sign up? They're your people. Treat them accordingly.
