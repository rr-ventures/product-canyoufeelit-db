# Title + Description Templates

## Title Formula

**[Emotion/State] + [Vibe/Genre] + [Format]**

### Examples:
- `when you finally let go | trip hop mix`
- `late night thoughts | lofi beats for feeling something`
- `the comfort of solitude | downtempo mix`
- `I miss who I used to be | emotional trip hop`
- `safe space for your feelings | 25min chill mix`
- `you don't need to explain | lofi for the restless`

### Rules:
- Max 60-70 characters (shows fully in search results)
- Lead with emotion/state (this is your hook)
- Use lowercase for intimacy (exception: proper nouns)
- No clickbaitâ€”deliver on the promise
- No dates/episode numbers in title (kills evergreen potential)

## SEO Notes

### What Matters:
1. **Title** â€” Primary ranking factor
2. **First 2 sentences of description** â€” Shows in search results
3. **Thumbnail CTR** â€” Affects ranking heavily
4. **Watch time** â€” Most important algo signal

### What Doesn't Matter (Much):
- Keyword stuffing in description
- Excessive tags (10-15 is plenty)
- Description length (keep it useful, not exhaustive)

### YouTube Search Behavior for This Niche:
- Users search for **feelings** not genres ("sad lofi mix" > "lofi hip hop beats")
- Long-tail keywords win ("late night study music for anxiety" > "study music")
- Evergreen > trending (your content should work in 2 years, not 2 weeks)
