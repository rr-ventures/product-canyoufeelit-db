# Newsletter Template

**Frequency**: Weekly or fortnightly (choose one and stick to it)

**Goal**: Share new finds + meaning. Not selling. Not promoting. Just resonance.

---

## Subject Line Formula

**[Feeling/State] + [What's Inside]**

### Examples:
- `3 tracks for when you're feeling too much`
- `this week's finds: solitude + longing`
- `new mix + the song that started it all`
- `for the quiet moments (+ this week's gems)`

**Rules**:
- Lowercase for intimacy
- No emoji (unless it really fits your brandâ€”test it)
- Promise value, not clickbait
- Max 50 characters

## Writing Tips

### Do:
- Write like you're texting a friend at 2am
- Be specific about feelings (not "sad"â€”"the kind of sad where you're grateful for the ache")
- Keep it short (300-400 words max)
- Use conversational formatting (short paragraphs, line breaks)
- Make it personal (share what YOU'RE feeling/listening to)

### Don't:
- Sell anything (not yetâ€”this is pure value for first 6 months)
- Use marketing jargon ("exclusive content," "don't miss out," etc.)
- Write formal paragraphs (this isn't a blog post)
- Over-explain tracks (2-3 sentences is enough)
- Batch-write 10 emails in advance (loses authenticityâ€”write week-of)

## Content Bank (When You're Stuck)

### Email Ideas:
1. **3 tracks for [specific feeling]**
2. **Behind the mix: How I chose the tracks for [Mix Name]**
3. **One song that changed everything for me**
4. **Tracks I've been sitting with this week**
5. **For when you need to feel something (+ 3 gems)**
6. **The mix I almost didn't upload (+ why I did)**
7. **Late-night listening report: [Theme]**

### When You Have Writer's Block:
- Pick ONE track that hit you hard this week
- Write 3 sentences about how it made you feel
- Add 2 more tracks that fit the vibe
- Send it

**Done is better than perfect.**

## Growth Strategy (Non-Spammy)

### In first 3 months:
- End each email with: *"Know someone who'd vibe with this? Forward it to them."*
- No referral programs, no incentivesâ€”just organic sharing

### After 100 subscribers:
- Occasionally ask: *"What are you feeling lately? Reply and I might build a mix around it."*
- Feature listener responses (with permission) in future emails

### After 500 subscribers:
- Consider a simple "mixtape request" form where subscribers submit a feeling/moment and you curate a short list of tracks

**Golden rule**: If it feels like marketing, delete it. This is a letter to friends, not a campaign.
